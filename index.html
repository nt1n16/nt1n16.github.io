Credit decision explanations

Assessing Criteria:

Assessing compliance

  • Is it easy to understand?
  • Is the information concise and tailored to the recipient?
  • Is it easily accessible?
  • Is it user friendly?
  • Is it provided in time for the recipient to act?
  • Is it complete?

Assessing effectiveness

  • Is the information relevant and accurate?
  • Does it allow the recipient to take action?
  • Does it allow the recipient to access their data?
  • Does it allow the recipient to correct a decision as appropriate?
  • Does it allow the recipient to provide feedback?
  • Does it integrate with existing processes?
